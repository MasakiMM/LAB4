College of Engineering and Information Technology

Programs

Graduate

Graduate programs in Information Systems prepare students to manage and utilize IT to solve business problems. Key areas include data management, system analysis, cybersecurity, and IT project management. Graduates often pursue careers as systems analysts, IT managers, and cybersecurity experts.

Learn more!

Undergraduate

Undergraduate programs in Information Systems provide students with foundational knowledge in managing and utilizing IT to address business needs. Core topics include database management, systems analysis, cybersecurity, and networking. Graduates often enter roles such as IT support specialists, systems analysts, or network administrators.

Learn more!

Certificates

Certificate programs in Information Systems offer focused, short-term training in specific areas like cybersecurity Informatics, Business Analytics, and Health IT. They are designed for quick skill acquisition and are ideal for career advancement or transitioning into IT roles.
